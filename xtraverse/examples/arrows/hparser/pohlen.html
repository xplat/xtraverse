HR Manager

Ref. Number: IS6754
Date: 28 Nov 2006

Dynamic Australasian Market Leader

 

Known for their inspirational leadership and values based culture this is a service driven business.  They are a market leader within their sector with a diverse multi site operation and in excess of 800 employees.

 

Reporting to the GM HR you will work as part of a team to help shape strategy and drive the implementation of a broad range of activities. Intrinsic to your success is the ability to support the long term direction of the organisation through coaching and business partnering.

 

We require someone who values integrity, complexity and team work. Your internal customers are diverse and sometimes challenging, so your relationship skills, resilience and flexibility will be critical.

 

Totally committed to delivering results you are an influencer able to balance a strategic perspective with the need to take a hands-on approach. 

 

So, if you are a proven performer able to hit the ground running, we would love to hear from you!

 

 

Please apply to Ruth Clavis or Beth Macdonald on-line by clicking on the above link, send written details to pk@pohlenkean.co.nz or mail to PO Box 91409, Auckland quoting reference IS6754.

A CV must accompany your application. Your application may be discussed with our client unless otherwise requested.

Only New Zealand residents or those permitted to work in New Zealand may apply for this role.
